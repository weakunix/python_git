Trade: 

There are 3 resources in the game:
Silk
Materials
Food

You can trade for these resources

Resources are important because you need them to:
Build (see Build and Upgrade)
Repair (see Build and Upgrade)
Keep the city happy (see City Happiness)

There will be a random trade deal every time

If you don't like the deal, you don't have to accept and the deal will change next time
